Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ShopReply — AI Auto-Reply for Facebook & Zalo ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that helps online shop owners automatically reply to customer messages using a local Q&A database and AI suggestions. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

ShopReply is a local-first application. All your data stays on your computer.

1. Data We Collect (Local Only)

The Extension processes and stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Customer messages — Incoming messages from Facebook Messenger and Zalo are read from the browser page (DOM) and sent to the local backend server running on your computer (localhost:3000). Messages never leave your machine.
  • Q&A database — Question-answer pairs you create or import are stored in a local SQLite database file on your computer.
  • Auto-reply logs — Records of automated replies (question, answer, confidence score, timestamp) are stored in the local SQLite database.
  • Extension settings — Preferences such as auto-reply threshold, tone settings, and enabled platforms are stored using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API.
  • Pro license key — If you purchase a Pro license, the key is stored locally for validation.

No customer data, messages, or Q&A content is ever sent to any external server.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Browsing history or URLs outside Facebook Messenger and Zalo
  • Analytics, telemetry, or usage statistics
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Data from any websites other than Facebook Messenger, Zalo, and localhost

3. Third-Party Services

LemonSqueezy (License Validation Only)

If you purchase a Pro license, the Extension sends your license key to the LemonSqueezy API (api.lemonsqueezy.com) solely to verify its validity. No customer data, messages, or Q&A content is transmitted. Only the license key string is sent. LemonSqueezy's own privacy policy applies to that interaction.

Ollama (Local LLM)

The Extension's backend uses Ollama for AI-powered features such as improving reply quality and analyzing chat history. Ollama runs entirely on your local machine — no data is sent to any cloud AI service.

No Other Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics or advertising services. There are no external API calls other than the optional LemonSqueezy license check described above.

4. Data Storage

  • SQLite database — Q&A pairs, auto-reply logs, and chat history are stored in a SQLite file on your computer, managed by the local backend server.
  • Chrome storage — Extension settings and state are stored using chrome.storage.local, which is sandboxed to the Extension and stays on your device.
  • Local backend — The backend server runs on localhost:3000 and is only accessible from your own computer. No ports are exposed to the network.

You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the Extension (removes Chrome storage data)
  • Deleting the backend data folder (removes SQLite database)
  • Clearing Extension storage from Chrome's extension settings

5. Permissions Explained

  • storage — Save your extension settings and connection state locally on your device.
  • tabs — Detect when you open Facebook Messenger or Zalo tabs to activate the auto-reply feature. No other tabs are accessed or tracked.
  • notifications — Show desktop notifications when new messages need your review, when the backend connection status changes, or when Q&A import completes.
  • host_permissions (facebook.com) — Read incoming customer messages and display the suggestion panel on Facebook Messenger pages. The Extension only activates on Messenger conversation pages.
  • host_permissions (chat.zalo.me) — Read incoming customer messages and display the suggestion panel on Zalo Web chat pages.
  • host_permissions (localhost) — Communicate with the local backend server running on your own computer. This connection never leaves your machine.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your customer conversations, Q&A database, and all other data remain exclusively on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
